Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LunaOS pricing

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

What's included in the free trial?

The 14-day free trial includes full access to all Pro features. No credit card required to start, and you can cancel anytime.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your payment in full.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees for any plan. You only pay the monthly or yearly subscription fee.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and PayPal through our secure payment processor.
